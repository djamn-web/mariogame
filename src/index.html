Mario Game

Check out my self programmed Mario Game in Phaser 3! :)

Help Mario navigate through self-designed levels filled with enemies and obstacles. Collect coins, stomp on Goombas, and try to finish by reaching the flag. Disclaimer: I do not own any of these images, they are used from the original Mario game.

Platform Game Phaser 3 JavaScript Multiple Levels

Quick Controls

  • ● Jump: Press W or Space (Double-click for Double Jump)
  • ● Move: Use Left/Right or A/D
  • ● Shoot: Press F or M to shoot enemies

Tip: Enable fullscreen mode and landscape mode to play on mobile devices!
